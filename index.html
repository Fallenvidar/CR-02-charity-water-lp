Our Story

Charity: water was founded in 2006 with a simple mission: bring clean and safe drinking water to every person on the planet. We started when our founder, Scott Harrison, learned that 1 billion people lacked access to clean water. Today, we're a global movement of students, donors, and partners creating transparent, data-driven solutions that bring clean, safe water to communities in need.

Every contribution is tracked. Every drop is measured. Every life is changed. Be part of something bigger than yourself and watch your impact grow in real-time. With 100% of public donations going directly to water projects, we've helped bring clean water to over 16 million people across 29 countries.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
